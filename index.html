Our House

Our guest house was built in 2017 and is sure to offer you a comfortable stay. It has 2 bedrooms, 1.5 baths, and new furnishings. Buena Vista, Colorado

Buena Vista is located right in the heart of the Sawatch Range of the Rockies in Central Colorado. At 8,000 ft with breathtaking views of Mt. Princeton, Yale, and Columbia, it will take your breath away in one way or another. With miles of trails accessible from River Park in town, or a short drive to many high country systems, you'll have no problem finding a hike, bike, or horse path. You can also catch a raft down the Ark from one of the many outfitters in town. The Ark Valley playground has no shortage of options regardless of your adrenaline tolerance.
